Renault car radio code generator

The calculation is done locally, in the browser, without sending the data to a server.
The free code is available in the GitHub repo.

The precode must be composed of a letter followed by 3 numbers (example : A123).
The precode is written on the back of the car radio, but can be recovered without removing it by simultaneously pressing keys 1 and 6 (or 1 and 5), then while holding them, press the power button.
The code calculation does not work for precodes starting with A0.
To activate your radio:
  1. Press times on 1.
  2. Press times on 2.
  3. Press times on 3.
  4. Press times on 4.
  5. To terminate press and hold 6 (or 5).
